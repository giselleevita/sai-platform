# SAI Platform — Pricing Model

## Value Metric

Per AI system registered per year. This aligns pricing with the unit of compliance value: each AI system requires its own risk assessment, policy rules, and audit trail.

## Tiers

| Tier             | Price                                                 | Includes                                                                                 |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Starter**      | $2,000/AI system/year (min 5 systems = $10,000/year)  | AI registry, risk scoring, policy rules, audit export, 2 admin users                     |
| **Professional** | $1,500/AI system/year (min 20 systems = $30,000/year) | Starter + vendor risk module, incident tracking, ML integration hooks, 10 users          |
| **Enterprise**   | Custom (50+ systems)                                  | Professional + SSO (SAML/OIDC), custom risk rubrics, dedicated CSM, SLA, unlimited users |

## Pilot Offer

- 60-day pilot: 5 AI systems registered at no cost
- Full platform access including risk scoring + audit export
- Conversion: auto-enroll in Starter at day 61 unless cancelled

## ROI Justification

- EU AI Act non-compliance fine: up to €35M or 7% of global annual turnover
- Cost of manual compliance tracking for 20 AI systems: 1–2 FTE compliance analysts ($150K–$300K/year)
- SAI Professional for 20 systems: $30,000/year — **80–90% cost reduction** vs. manual
